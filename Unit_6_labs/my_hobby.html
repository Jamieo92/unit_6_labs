My Favourite Hobbie is Golf

One of my favourite things to do in my spare time is play golf. I feel it is the best way to switch off from the world for a couple of hours. I try get out to play at least once a week if the weather is good. I like to try out new courses all the time and it is a good way to travel and see the country.

I am a member of lisheen springs golf club which has some amazing scenery and some fantastic holes.
They host club competitions every Monday and Wednesday which I try to play as much as i can because the competitions are the best way to improve your game.
